About Us

What would you do if you heard your daughter or sister has been raped? Just sit down and keep silent? In every 16 minutes, a woman was raped in India in 2019 alone. This woman could be your daughter, your sister or your friend. These worrying and disturbing events laid the foundation of NaariSamatā.

NaariSamatā is a charitable organisation founded to work on the prevention of rape through youth awareness initiatives. It teaches the youth about gender equality and respect for women.

The organisation was officially registered in Britain on 16th June, 2021 under the registration number 1194814.

We campaign for the safety of women, children, the under privileged and the marginalised communities. We work on issues related to gender, caste and sexuality. To build a safe world, we teach the youth about equality and mutual respect as a way of fighting violence against women. At our core is the empowerment of women and girls in India.

"Since most sexual abuse begins well before puberty, preventive education, if it is to have any effect at all, should begin early in middle school" - Judith Lewis Herman

Act now to help us make this world a better place.

OUR VISION

A safe world for all where equality is the norm not a privilege.

OUR OBJECTIVES

As defined in our constitution:

- To promote gender equality and diversity for the public benefit.

- To eliminate discrimination on the grounds of gender and age.

- To advance education through the creation of e-learning tools and resources.

- To promote the welfare of children and young adults in under privileged communities by providing recreational facilities.

- To provide support and advice to women and children who have experienced or are at risk of experiencing violence and abuse.

- To help vulnerable adults, children and young people in under privileged communities by supporting activities which develop them into mature and responsible people.
